    THE REVOLUTION OF SIRAAS

    SIRaaS stands for smart interactive receipt as a service.

    When compared with paper and digital receipts of today, SIRAAS is a receipt revolution on two main fronts

    1-Receipt Security Revolution
    2-Receipt Operational Revolution

    Receipt Security Revolution

    Paper and digital receipts are often exchanged between people, organizations, courts and Governments as a proof of purchase or a proof of payment.

    When people, organizations and Governments receive paper or digital receipts, they can not prove on the fly if these receipts are authentic, counterfeit or if they truly belong to their senders.

    When it comes down to paper receipts, anyone can grab a paper receipt and claim it is theirs. Digital receipts, which come in various digital formats are easy to tamper with, using common and freely available software tools.

    With SIRAAS, receipts are digitally signed by their issuers and cryptographically validated by their recipients to ensure that every receipt is authentic and truly belongs to its sender.

    RECEIPT SECURITY REVOLUTION

    SIRAAS is a real proof of purchase, because every receipt gets cryptographically validated by its recipient.

    Receipt Operational Revolution

    When we receive emails, we can easily perform common email related actions such as “Reply”, “Forward” and “Delete” from the email itself. We perform such actions in the same sequence and manner, regardless of who the email sender is.

    SIRAAS App is a messenger service just for receipts. When shoppers receive receipts from any retailer, they can perform receipt related actions such as Returns, Expenses, Tips, Bill Splits and more from the receipt itself.

    RECEIPT SECURITY REVOLUTION

    SIRAAS enables retailers, organizations and governments to bridge their process and policy with the receipts that they send and receive.

    The result is a universe of digitization, automation and real-time analytics that tie payments to people, products and services in accordance with policy, rules and regulations.

    SIRAAS delivers superior capabilities to shoppers, retailers and organizations in ways that paper and digital receipts were not designed to achieve.

    SIRaaS is a smart interactive receipt as a service.

    Receipts exist in every retailer, household, company and Government. SIRaaS delivers unmatched value for receipt issuers and recipients.
