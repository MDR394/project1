    SIRAAS RETAILER DIGITIZATION

    Shoppers usually need their receipts to perform tasks like returns, expenses, tips, warranty claims and others.

    Shoppers no longer need to spend time and effort to search for receipts, take pictures of receipts and to craft emails that include receipts. With SIRaaS, shoppers can now perform receipt related tasks straight from their smart receipts, with just a single click.

    In addition to centralizing receipts and simplifying their operations, SIRaaS allow shoppers to gain points for spending, giving feedback and referring others.

    Why should shoppers use SIRaaS?

    Convenience

    Perform all tasks that require receipts from the receipt itself.
    (Example: returns, tips, expense bill, split bill, etc.)

    Biggest Gains (3-way gains)

    Gain points when you spend, give feedback & refer others from your receipt.

    Centralized content, rewards & points

    -A Messenger-like app for all receipts from all stores.

    -Collect, redeem and transfer points between friends and family for all stores from 1 app.

    SIRaaS is a smart interactive receipt as a service.

    Receipts exist in every retailer, household, company and Government. SIRaaS delivers unmatched value for receipt issuers and recipients.
